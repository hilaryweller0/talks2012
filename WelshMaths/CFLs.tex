\heading{Typical Maximum Courant numbers}

Horizontal Courant number, $=c\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x}$\\ \ \\
Vertical Courant number,   $=c\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta z}$\\ \ \\
where $c$ is the advection or wave speed \\ \ \\
